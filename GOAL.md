# Goal

Users should be able to look up any politician or candidate, then see all the bills
they voted on unrestricted, all the actions they made outside of bills (to see if
they were virtue signaling) and then be able to assess what they actually voted for
in comparison to what they said or say they will do vs. what they said on the
campaign trail, vs. what their constituents actually want. We need to be able to see
the outcomes of those bills, how they were executed, if any issues arose because of
the bill writing, or executive branch or judiciary branch actions or inability to
execute or appropriate funds, if the bills got pocket vetoed, if they were
filibustered and by who — basically a watchdog that tracks everything a politician
does.

---

We should also be able to see what funding sources people have so that we can be
transparent.

---

District by district polling is nice (even exit polling) because that would allow
for proper constituent sentiment comparison.

---

This app should be able to hold thousands of users even without setting up accounts,
but if they want they can set up an account to track specific politicians and get
email updates.

It needs to be able to update at least once a day or week
(once a day during the last month prior to election, of course).

---

It should track what politicians are up for election as well, how long they've been
in Congress even if we don't pull their voting history.

---

It should also track net worth, how much money they have made over their term,
graphed, and then spread out among what were the income sources including stocks.

---
